About Our Event

The City of Bayjing won the bid to host the next FunOlympic Games in 2022. During the build- up to the planned games in 2022 the city had already invested hugely in IT infrastructure and systems to make sure that the games would have been well organised, staffed, attended, and accessible to everyone. The FunOlympic committee have decided that they require a number of additional projects which need to be completed before the games start to ensure the games run smoothly and also that worldwide audiences can enjoy the games online.
